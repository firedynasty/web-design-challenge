Cities' Latitude vs. Humidity

There is very low correlation between humidity and the latitude of the cities. As you can see there is high and low humidity between the cities between the equator and the North and South Poles. It is worth nothing that the r-squared correlation value is 0.08 for Northern Hemisphere Cities trending upwards. The farther away from the equator going North correlates with a higher humidity.
